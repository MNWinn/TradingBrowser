ROBUSTNESS TESTING METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

Walk-Forward Optimization
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Window Count: 5 windows per configuration
  • Train/Test Split: 60% in-sample, 40% out-of-sample
  • Overlap: Non-overlapping windows for true out-of-sample testing
  
  Purpose:
    Walk-forward optimization tests strategy stability across time,
    preventing curve-fitting to specific historical periods.

Multiple Dataset Validation
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Dataset Count: 3 synthetic datasets
  • Dataset Characteristics:
    - Dataset 1: Moderate trend (0.01% drift), 2% volatility
    - Dataset 2: Strong trend (0.02% drift), 2.5% volatility
    - Dataset 3: Weak trend (-0.01% drift), 1.5% volatility
  
  Purpose:
    Testing across multiple datasets with different characteristics
    ensures strategy robustness to varying market regimes.

Robustness Criteria
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  All strategies must meet the following criteria to pass:
  
  ✓ Minimum Trades: ≥ 20 (ensures statistical significance)
  ✓ Minimum Win Rate: ≥ 45% (ensures positive expectancy)
  ✓ Maximum Drawdown: ≤ 25% (ensures risk control)
  ✓ Minimum Sharpe: ≥ 0.3 (ensures risk-adjusted returns)
  ✓ Minimum Profit Factor: ≥ 1.1 (ensures profitability)

Scoring Methodology
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Overall Score (0-100) calculated as:
    • Win Rate Score: min(win_rate / 70% * 20, 20)
    • Return Score: min(max(return, 0) / 50% * 25, 25)
    • Sharpe Score: min(max(sharpe, 0) / 2.0 * 20, 20)
    • Drawdown Score: max(0, 15 - drawdown / 20% * 15)
    • Profit Factor Score: min(max(PF - 1, 0) / 2.0 * 10, 10)

APPENDIX: STRATEGY DEFINITIONS

ATR Breakout
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Long Entry:  Close > SMA(20) + 2.0 * ATR(14)
  Short Entry: Close < SMA(20) - 2.0 * ATR(14)
  Stop Loss:   Entry Price * (1 ± 2%)
  Take Profit: Entry Price * (1 ± 3%)  [1.5:1 ratio]

EMA Crossover (12/26)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Long Entry:  EMA(12) crosses above EMA(26)
  Short Entry: EMA(12) crosses below EMA(26)
  Stop Loss:   Entry Price * (1 ± 2%)
  Take Profit: Entry Price * (1 ± 4%)  [2:1 ratio]

MACD (8/21/5)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Long Entry:  MACD Line > Signal Line AND Histogram > 0
  Short Entry: MACD Line < Signal Line AND Histogram < 0
  Stop Loss:   Entry Price * (1 ± 2%)
  Take Profit: Entry Price * (1 ± 4%)  [2:1 ratio]

RSI Mean Reversion (30/70)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Long Entry:  RSI(14) < 30 (oversold)
  Short Entry: RSI(14) > 70 (overbought)
  Stop Loss:   Entry Price * (1 ± 2%)
  Take Profit: Entry Price * (1 ± 4%)  [2:1 ratio]
